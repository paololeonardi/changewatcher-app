Privacy Policy — ChangeWatcher

This Privacy Policy explains how ChangeWatcher (the “App”) handles your information.

Summary

  • The App connects only to the changedetection.io service that you configure
  • Third-party SDKs are used for advertising, purchases, crash reporting, and performance monitoring
  • No App-specific account is required

Information the App Handles

Information you provide:

  • Service URL or host address, and credentials or tokens to access your changedetection.io instance
  • Credentials are stored securely in your device’s keychain

Information from your changedetection.io service:

  • Watch names, monitored URLs, statuses, results, and related metadata
  • This information is handled locally on your device and cached for display purposes
  • Used to connect to and operate with your configured service

Device and usage information (handled via third-party SDKs):

  • Device identifiers, interaction events, diagnostics, crash and performance data
  • Processed by third-party services (listed below) for advertising, purchases, crash diagnostics, and performance monitoring

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for advertising
  • RevenueCat for subscriptions and entitlements
  • Sentry for crash diagnostics
  • New Relic for performance monitoring

These providers process data such as device identifiers, IP addresses, diagnostics, and events under their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

The App primarily acts as a client to your own changedetection.io service. Data is shared:

  • With your changedetection.io service: when you initiate requests to fetch and display your watches
  • With third-party SDK providers: as described above for their respective purposes

We do not collect or sell your personal information.

Data Retention

  • Cached service data remains on your device; uninstalling the App removes it
  • SDK diagnostic and performance data is retained according to each provider’s policy

Your Rights & Controls

You have the following options:

  • Adjust advertising privacy settings on your device in iOS settings
  • Disable the App’s network access (note that the App requires network access to function)
  • Uninstall the App to remove local data
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data

Security

We take the following security measures:

  • Credentials and tokens you enter are stored using your device’s keychain
  • Network requests use HTTPS where available
  • We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please also take steps to secure your changedetection.io service and credentials.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, or under 16 where required by law (such as in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under the applicable age, please do not use the App.

International Transfers

Third-party providers may process your data on servers located in other countries.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the App after such updates means you accept the changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App or the App Store listing.
